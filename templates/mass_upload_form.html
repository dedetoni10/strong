CSV Format Requirements
Required Columns:
  • name - Product name
  • sku - Stock keeping unit
  • quantity - Stock quantity
  • price - Product price
Optional Columns:
  • minimum_stock - Minimum stock level (default: 10)
Tip: Make sure your CSV file has headers and all required columns are present.
